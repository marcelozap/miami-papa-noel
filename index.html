The Story

A polished Santa experience with a community heart.

Miami Papa Noel began in 2017 when a Metro by T-Mobile office wanted Christmas activities for children visiting the store. What started as a photo backdrop became a tradition: novenas, gift deliveries, family parties, business events, residential communities, and nonprofit collaborations.

He has supported activities connected with Doral Club and the University of Miami Mailman Center for Child Development. The work matters most when it brings children comfort, wonder, and a memory their family can hold onto.

FAQ

Answers before they ask.

Professional event pages reduce back-and-forth and help the family book faster.

Do you serve outside Doral?

Yes. Miami Papa Noel serves Miami-Doral and nearby areas including Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, and Coral Gables. Travel may affect pricing.

Can Santa deliver gifts?

Yes. Gifts must be provided by the family or organizer and clearly labeled with each child's name.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. The date is confirmed only after deposit, event details, and calendar confirmation.

Can nonprofits book a visit?

Yes. Nonprofit, toy drive, and sponsor-supported community options may be available depending on date and event needs.
