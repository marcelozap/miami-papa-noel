The Story

A Santa tradition with a big community heart.

Miami Papa Noel began in 2017 when a Metro by T-Mobile office wanted Christmas activities for children visiting the store. What started as a simple photo backdrop became a tradition: novenas, gift deliveries, family parties, business events, residential communities, and nonprofit moments.

He has supported activities connected with Doral Club and the University of Miami Mailman Center for Child Development. The best part is still the same: seeing children feel surprised, comfortable, and happy.

FAQ

Good to know.

Quick answers before you send a request.

Do you serve outside Doral?

Yes. Miami Papa Noel serves Miami-Doral and nearby areas including Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, and Coral Gables. Travel may affect pricing.

Can Santa deliver gifts?

Yes. Gifts must be provided by the family or organizer and clearly labeled with each child's name.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. The date is confirmed only after deposit, event details, and calendar confirmation.

Can nonprofits book a visit?

Yes. Nonprofit, toy drive, and sponsor-supported community options may be available depending on date and event needs.
