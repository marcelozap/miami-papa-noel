His Story

A Santa with real community roots.

It started in 2017, when a Metro by T-Mobile office wanted to create Christmas activities for children visiting the store. The idea was so well received that a Christmas photo backdrop was purchased, and families began asking for more.

Over time, Papa Noel began appearing at Christmas novenas, private family celebrations, residential communities, businesses, nonprofit events, and Christmas Eve gift deliveries. He has collaborated with community activities connected with Doral Club and the University of Miami Mailman Center for Child Development.
