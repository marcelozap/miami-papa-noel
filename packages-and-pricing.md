# Packages and Pricing

These are suggested ranges. Adjust based on travel distance, date, time, and demand.

## Family Visit

Best for homes, birthdays, small family parties, and surprise visits.

- 20-30 minutes.
- Photos with children and family.
- Short greeting from Santa.
- Optional family-provided gift handoff.
- Suggested public starting price: $125+.

## Event Visit

Best for larger family parties, restaurants, churches, small business events, schools, daycares, and community events.

- 45-60 minutes.
- Photos with guests.
- Santa greeting and interaction.
- Gift handoff if gifts are prepared and labeled.
- Suggested public starting price: $225+.

Larger residential communities, schools, business activations, and long events should be quoted higher after details are reviewed.

## Christmas Eve Gift Delivery

Best for high-value bookings on December 24.

- Santa arrives with family-provided gifts.
- Short magical visit and photos.
- Time windows should be tightly scheduled.
- Suggested public starting price: $250+ for short visit, higher for prime evening times.

## Nonprofit and Toy Drive Visit

Best for community events and organizations serving children.

- Discounted or sponsored rate.
- Sponsor can be mentioned in posts.
- Suggested structure: paid by sponsor, discounted for nonprofit, or donated when possible.

## Add-Ons

- Extra time.
- Travel outside core area.
- Christmas Eve premium.
- Photo backdrop setup.
- Assistant/elf coordination.
- Bilingual announcement or short story time.

## Booking Terms

- Require deposit to hold date and time.
- Final payment due before or at arrival.
- Client provides parking instructions.
- Gifts must be clearly labeled if Santa is handing them out.
- For public events, client manages line, crowd flow, and child safety.
