## Purpose

Miami Papa Noel is a family-owned Santa visit business for Miami-Doral and nearby areas.

The business exists to help Dad turn his trusted Papa Noel experience into real bookings: family visits, photos, Christmas Eve gift delivery, schools, daycares, businesses, HOAs, residential communities, toy drives, nonprofits, Christmas novenas, and Summer Santa / Christmas in July.

The brand should feel:

- Warm
- Local
- Bilingual
- Family-owned
- Trustworthy with children
- Photo-ready
- Professional, but not corporate

## Short Story

Miami Papa Noel started around 2017 when a Metro by T-Mobile office wanted Christmas activities for children visiting the store. A Christmas backdrop was purchased for photos, families loved it, and the visits grew into novenas, Christmas Eve gift deliveries, family parties, residential community events, business events, and nonprofit/community activities.

The community side matters most. The goal is not to look like a big agency. The goal is to look real, warm, experienced, and easy to book.
