# Sales Funnel

## Goal

Turn local families, schools, businesses, residential communities, and nonprofits into paid bookings.

## Funnel Stages

### 1. Awareness

People discover Miami Papa Noel through:

- Instagram posts and reels
- Facebook community groups
- WhatsApp shares
- Google Business Profile
- Referrals from past clients
- Partnerships with photographers, decorators, party rentals, daycares, and HOAs

Main message:

`Book a warm bilingual Santa visit for your family, school, business, or community event.`

### 2. Interest

Send people to:

- Website homepage
- `https://miamipapanoel.com/book`
- `https://miamipapanoel.com/partners` for referral partners and vendor conversations
- Instagram DM
- WhatsApp Business

They should immediately see:

- Real photos
- Experience since 2017
- Bilingual service
- Event types
- Easy request button

### 3. Inquiry

Collect:

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Date
- Time
- City
- Event type
- Guest count
- Gift details
- What Santa should do

### 4. Quote

Reply with:

- Availability
- Recommended package
- Price
- Deposit required
- Remaining balance
- What is included
- What client must prepare

### 5. Booking

Booking is not confirmed until:

- Date/time are agreed
- Deposit is paid
- Address and parking are received
- Gift instructions are clear

### 6. Pre-Event Confirmation

Send confirmation 48 hours before:

- Arrival time
- Address
- Parking
- Contact person
- Gift labels
- Photo permission
- Balance due

### 7. Event

Capture:

- 2-5 photos or short clips if client allows
- Testimonial opportunity
- Referral opportunity

### 8. Review and Referral

Within 24 hours:

- Thank client
- Ask for review
- Ask if they know another family, school, business, or HOA

## Daily Sales Routine

- 15 Instagram DMs
- 5 Facebook/WhatsApp messages
- 5 emails
- 3 phone calls
- 5 follow-ups
- 1 story post
- 1 feed post or reel every other day

## Weekly Targets

- 100 new leads contacted
- 25 follow-ups
- 10 quote conversations
- 3-5 deposits
- 2 referral partners
- 1 partner outreach block completed using `referral-partner-playbook.md`
