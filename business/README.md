# Miami Papa Noel Business Kit

This folder turns the website into an operating system for the business.

## Use These First

1. `fathers-day-launch.html` - polished handoff page for Father's Day with status, roles, next steps, and printable QR booking card.
2. `email-form-finish.md` - exact final steps for MX records, business email forwarding, and FormSubmit activation.
3. `gemini-marketing-kit.md` - paste-ready Gemini prompts for captions, replies, outreach, Google posts, and content calendars.
4. `passive-marketing-engine.md` - one-time setup system for Google, marketplaces, scheduled posts, reviews, and automated booking leads.
5. `directory-profile-kit.md` - paste-ready profile copy for Google Business Profile, GigSalad, The Bash, Instagram, Facebook, Yelp, and local directories.
6. `launch-command-center.html` - internal dashboard for outreach, scripts, tracking links, and follow-ups.
7. `summer-client-engine.md` - the active June/July client-acquisition plan for camps, daycares, HOAs, pet businesses, partners, and toy drives.
8. `summer-prospect-starter.csv` - starter prospect list to research and contact first.
9. `quote-builder.html` - internal tool for turning inquiry details into a polished text, WhatsApp, or email quote.
10. `follow-up-builder.html` - internal tool for turning warm leads, quiet leads, proof requests, deposit reminders, partners, and completed visits into clear next-step messages.
11. `account-setup-checklist.md` - create the accounts.
12. `business-email-setup.md` - create the professional email address.
13. `brand-profile.md` - paste-ready bios and descriptions.
14. `sales-funnel.md` - how strangers become paid bookings.
15. `booking-sop.md` - exact process from inquiry to event.
16. `launch-checklist.md` - what to do in the first 7 days.
17. `30-day-client-sprint.md` - daily outreach routine and bilingual messages.
18. `local-lead-research-playbook.md` - how to build a real local lead list.
19. `referral-partner-playbook.md` - how to contact photographers, vendors, pet businesses, restaurants, and local partners.
20. `../links.html` - quick link-in-bio page for Instagram and social profiles.
21. `../events.html` - outreach landing page for schools, HOAs, businesses, toy drives, and event partners.
22. `../christmas-eve.html` - focused Christmas Eve gift-delivery page for families.
23. `../summer-santa.html` - focused Summer Santa and Christmas-in-July page for off-season bookings.
24. `../service-areas.html` - local service-area page for Miami, Doral, Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, Coral Gables, and nearby communities.
25. `../schools-daycares.html` - focused school, daycare, classroom, and camp visit page.
26. `../hoa-apartments.html` - focused HOA, apartment, condo, clubhouse, and resident event page.
27. `../partners.html` - referral-partner page for photographers, vendors, pet businesses, restaurants, and local family businesses.
28. `../reviews.html` - proof page with real visit photos, community notes, and review collection guidance.
29. `../after-visit.html` - thank-you page for reviews, approved photo permission, and referrals after completed visits.
30. `review-referral-system.md` - post-visit review, photo permission, and referral workflow.
31. `one-page-flyer.html` - printable flyer for families, schools, HOAs, and businesses.
32. `google-social-launch-kit.md` - paste-ready Google Business Profile, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, and weekly growth checklist.
33. `google-business-profile-launch.md` - paste-ready Google Business Profile setup pack.

## Core Rule

Every lead should move to one of these statuses:

- New lead
- Waiting for details
- Price sent
- Deposit requested
- Booked
- Completed
- Review requested
- Referral requested
