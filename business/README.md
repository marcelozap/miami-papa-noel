# Miami Papa Noel Business Kit

This folder turns the website into an operating system for the business.

## Use These First

1. `launch-command-center.html` - daily internal dashboard for outreach, scripts, tracking links, and follow-ups.
2. `account-setup-checklist.md` - create the accounts.
3. `business-email-setup.md` - create the professional email address.
4. `brand-profile.md` - paste-ready bios and descriptions.
5. `sales-funnel.md` - how strangers become paid bookings.
6. `booking-sop.md` - exact process from inquiry to event.
7. `launch-checklist.md` - what to do in the first 7 days.
8. `30-day-client-sprint.md` - daily outreach routine and bilingual messages.
9. `local-lead-research-playbook.md` - how to build a real local lead list.
10. `referral-partner-playbook.md` - how to contact photographers, vendors, pet businesses, restaurants, and local partners.
11. `../links.html` - quick link-in-bio page for Instagram and social profiles.
12. `../events.html` - outreach landing page for schools, HOAs, businesses, toy drives, and event partners.
13. `../christmas-eve.html` - focused Christmas Eve gift-delivery page for families.
14. `../summer-santa.html` - focused Summer Santa and Christmas-in-July page for off-season bookings.
15. `../service-areas.html` - local service-area page for Miami, Doral, Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, Coral Gables, and nearby communities.
16. `../schools-daycares.html` - focused school, daycare, classroom, and camp visit page.
17. `../hoa-apartments.html` - focused HOA, apartment, condo, clubhouse, and resident event page.
18. `../partners.html` - referral-partner page for photographers, vendors, pet businesses, restaurants, and local family businesses.
19. `../reviews.html` - proof page with real visit photos, community notes, and review collection guidance.
20. `../after-visit.html` - thank-you page for reviews, approved photo permission, and referrals after completed visits.
21. `review-referral-system.md` - post-visit review, photo permission, and referral workflow.
22. `one-page-flyer.html` - printable flyer for families, schools, HOAs, and businesses.
23. `google-social-launch-kit.md` - paste-ready Google Business Profile, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, and weekly growth checklist.
24. `google-business-profile-launch.md` - paste-ready Google Business Profile setup pack.

## Core Rule

Every lead should move to one of these statuses:

- New lead
- Waiting for details
- Price sent
- Deposit requested
- Booked
- Completed
- Review requested
- Referral requested
