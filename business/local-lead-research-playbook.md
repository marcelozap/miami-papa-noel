## Qualification Checklist

Add a lead if at least two of these are true:

- They serve families, children, residents, donors, or local shoppers.
- They post events, promotions, family activities, or holiday content.
- They are in Miami, Doral, Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, Coral Gables, or nearby.
- They have active Instagram/Facebook posts from the last 60 days.
- They have a public phone, email, website form, or DM button.
- A Santa visit would help them create photos, attendance, donations, or customer goodwill.

Skip a lead if:

- Their page looks inactive.
- They are far outside the service area.
- They have no clear way to contact them.
- The audience is not family, children, community, holiday, retail, or event related.

## Daily Lead Building Routine

### 30 minutes: find leads

Pick one category and one area. Example: `Doral daycares`.

Add 10 leads to `lead-tracker.csv` with:

- Business Name
- Type
- Area
- Instagram or website
- Phone or email if public
- Package Interest
- Lead Source
- Next Action

### 30 minutes: send outreach

Send 10 DMs or emails using the matching script in `30-day-client-sprint.md`.

Personal first-line examples:

- "I saw you host family activities in Doral."
- "Your holiday photos from last year looked beautiful."
- "This looks like a sweet fit for your residents."
- "I saw your daycare does seasonal activities for the children."
- "Your family photo sessions look like a great fit for a Santa mini-session."

### 10 minutes: log follow-up dates

Set follow-up date 2 days later for every contacted lead.

Use these statuses:

- New lead
- Contacted
- Interested
- Sent options
- Waiting for date/time
- Deposit needed
- Booked
- Not this year

## Follow-Up Rhythm

Day 0: first message.  
Day 2: friendly follow-up.  
Day 7: send the reviews page or flyer.
Day 14: ask if they are planning December dates yet.  
Early November: urgency follow-up for December weekends.  
Early December: final availability follow-up.

## Weekly Target

- 70 new leads added.
- 50 first messages sent.
- 25 follow-ups sent.
- 5 warm conversations.
- 1-3 booked or likely bookings.

## Simple Rule

Every conversation should end with one next step:

- Send photos
- Send visit options
- Ask for date/time
- Ask for city/guest count
- Send price
- Request deposit
- Follow up on a specific date
