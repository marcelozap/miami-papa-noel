## Photo Upload Order

Upload these before doing heavy outreach:

1. Strong full-body Santa photo.
2. Santa by a decorated Christmas tree.
3. Family or child photo with permission.
4. Community event group photo with permission.
5. Gift handoff or Christmas Eve-style moment.
6. Pet photo, if promoting pet photos.
7. Printed card or event proof.
8. One warm close-up where the costume and expression are clear.

Do not upload blurry screenshots, photos without permission, unrelated phone screenshots, or anything that feels unfinished.

## Weekly Growth Routine

Do this every week from now through December:

- Add 1-2 approved photos to Google Business Profile.
- Post 1 Google update.
- Post 2 Instagram/Facebook feed posts.
- Post 3-5 stories.
- Message 50 local leads from the tracker.
- Follow up with every quote that has not replied.
- Ask every completed visit for a review or approved photo.
- Add every inquiry to `../lead-tracker.csv` with the lead source.

## What To Track

Add these to the lead tracker for every inquiry:

- Lead source: Instagram, Google Business Profile, Facebook, referral, service areas page, partners page, school page, HOA page, or reviews page.
- Visit type requested.
- City.
- Date.
- Status.
- Next follow-up date.

The booking form already includes a `Lead source` line when the visitor comes through a tracked link.
