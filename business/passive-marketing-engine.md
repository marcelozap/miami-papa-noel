# Miami Papa Noel Passive Marketing Engine

This is the low-maintenance version of the business plan: set up the places where people already search for Santa, send every click to the booking page, and let inquiries arrive by email, text, or WhatsApp.

The goal is not "post every day." The goal is that Mom and Dad receive warm inquiries without needing to manage a marketing routine.

## The Lead Flow

1. A parent, school, HOA, daycare, or business searches for a Santa in Miami.
2. They find Miami Papa Noel on Google, Instagram, Facebook, GigSalad, The Bash, or a referral link.
3. Every profile points to `https://miamipapanoel.com/book` with a source tag.
4. The booking form sends the request to `bookings@miamipapanoel.com`.
5. The customer lands on `https://miamipapanoel.com/thank-you` with text and WhatsApp as urgent backups.
6. After a completed visit, they get the review/referral page: `https://miamipapanoel.com/after-visit`.

## Google Business Profile Setup

Use Google Business Profile because Google says it can put a free profile on Search and Maps, and lets the business add photos, posts, offers, and profile details.

Recommended profile fields:

- Business name: `Miami Papa Noel`
- Category to try first: `Entertainer`
- Secondary categories if available: `Party entertainment service`, `Event planner`, `Photographer`
- Service area: `Doral, Miami, Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, Coral Gables, and nearby Miami-Dade areas`
- Website: `https://miamipapanoel.com`
- Appointment link: `https://miamipapanoel.com/book?source=google%20business`
- Phone: `305-244-0360`
- Description: use the copy in `directory-profile-kit.md`
- Photos: upload 10 to 15 real photos first, with the strongest children/family/community photos at the top.

Do not use a home address publicly unless the family wants that. A service-area business is safer for private visits.

## Review System

After the Google profile is verified, use Google's review link or QR code feature. Google says the Business Profile can provide a review link and a QR code from the review section.

Use reviews only from real clients and real experiences. Do not pay for reviews, trade discounts for reviews, or ask anyone to write something they did not experience.

Simple after-visit text:


## Meta Scheduled Posts

Meta Business Suite can schedule posts in advance. Instead of asking Mom and Dad to post all the time, schedule 8 to 12 posts in one sitting:

- 3 family visit posts
- 2 school/daycare posts
- 2 HOA/business event posts
- 2 Summer Santa / Christmas in July posts
- 2 Christmas Eve gift delivery posts
- 1 nonprofit/community story post

Every caption should end with one link:

`https://miamipapanoel.com/book?source=instagram`

or

`https://miamipapanoel.com/book?source=facebook`

## The No-Daily-Work Operating Rule

The family only needs to do these tiny actions:

- Dad: show up, perform warmly, and tell people they can text the number.
- Mom: only needs to approve important wording or photos if she wants.
- You and your sister: set up profiles, schedule posts, and check the booking inbox.

No one should be manually chasing posts every day.
