# Offer and Pricing Framework

Do not publish exact prices until the family agrees. Use ranges privately until you learn demand.

## Suggested Packages

### Mini Visit

Use for:

- Homes
- Birthdays
- Quick surprise visits
- Small photo moments

Includes:

- 20-30 minutes
- Photos
- Short greeting
- Optional gift handoff

Suggested range:

`$100-$175`

### Party Visit

Use for:

- Family parties
- Restaurants
- Churches
- Small business events

Includes:

- 45-60 minutes
- Photos
- Guest greetings
- Gift handoff if prepared

Suggested range:

`$175-$300`

### Community Event

Use for:

- HOAs
- Apartment communities
- Schools
- Daycares
- Business events
- Toy drives

Includes:

- 1-2 hours
- Photo line
- Guest interaction
- Event support

Suggested range:

`$300-$650`

### Christmas Eve Delivery

Use for:

- Family gift delivery
- Short high-impact visit

Includes:

- Scheduled arrival window
- Gift delivery
- Photos
- Short visit

Suggested range:

`$175-$350+`

## Add Premiums

Add more for:

- Christmas Eve evening
- Long travel
- Last-minute booking
- Events over 1 hour
- Large guest count
- Parking difficulty
- Multiple stops

## Deposit

Recommended:

- 30%-50% deposit
- Non-refundable unless Papa Noel cancels
- Remaining balance due before or at arrival

## Discount Strategy

Do discounts only for:

- Nonprofits
- Toy drives
- Weekday events
- Early-bird deposits
- Referral partners

Do not discount prime December weekends too much.
