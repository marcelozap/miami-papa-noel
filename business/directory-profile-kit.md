## Long Bio

Miami Papa Noel offers private Santa visits for families, schools, daycares, businesses, residential communities, Christmas parties, toy drives, Christmas in July events, and Christmas Eve gift delivery across Miami-Doral and nearby areas.

What began in 2017 with holiday photos for children at a local Metro by T-Mobile office grew into Christmas novenas, private gift deliveries, community events, nonprofit visits, and holiday appearances with entertainment groups. The work that matters most to him is bringing joy to children and supporting family-centered community events.

He is bilingual in English and Spanish, warm with children, comfortable in photo settings, and available for simple home visits or larger organized events.
