# 30-Day Client Sprint

Goal: create steady conversations with families, schools, HOAs, daycares, businesses, photographers, event planners, and nonprofits before December dates fill.

## Daily Minimum

- Send 10 Instagram or Facebook DMs.
- Send 5 emails.
- Make 2 calls or WhatsApp messages.
- Follow up with 3 people from earlier conversations.
- Post 1 story or photo.
- Log every lead in `../lead-tracker.csv`.

## Weekly Focus

### Week 1: Warm Proof

- Post the best 9 photos.
- Ask past clients or nonprofit contacts for one short review.
- Send the reviews page when anyone asks for photos or proof: https://miamipapanoel.com/reviews
- Message family photographers, balloon decorators, party rentals, and event planners.
- Offer referral partnerships.
- Send partner leads here: https://miamipapanoel.com/partners

### Week 2: Family and School Leads

- Contact daycares, preschools, elementary PTAs, churches, and kids activity centers.
- Promote home visits, classroom greetings, story time, and photo moments.
- Use gentle wording: "warm bilingual Papa Noel visit" and "good with shy children."

### Week 3: Communities and Businesses

- Contact HOAs, apartment communities, shopping plazas, restaurants, salons, pediatric offices, and kids dental offices.
- Offer photo line support, customer appreciation events, and family days.
- Mention that Christmas Eve and December weekends are limited.

### Week 4: Urgency and Follow-Up

- Follow up with every warm lead.
- Share available weekend windows.
- Ask if they need family visit, event visit, or Christmas Eve gift delivery.
- Offer to hold a date only after details and deposit are confirmed.
