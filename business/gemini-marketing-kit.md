# Gemini Marketing Kit

Use these prompts in Gemini when you want quick marketing help without starting from a blank page.

Important: Gemini can help write and organize marketing. It cannot activate `bookings@miamipapanoel.com`, add MX records, verify FormSubmit, or complete Google Business Profile ownership by itself.

```text
You are helping market Miami Papa Noel, a warm bilingual Santa serving Miami-Doral and nearby areas since 2017.

Services:
- Private family Santa visits
- Photos with Santa
- Christmas Eve gift delivery
- Schools, daycares, camps, HOAs, apartments, businesses, toy drives, nonprofits
- Summer Santa / Christmas in July
- Pet photos with Santa

Tone:
Warm, local, family-owned, professional, bilingual English/Spanish, not corporate.

Website:
https://miamipapanoel.com

Booking:
https://miamipapanoel.com/book

Photos/proof:
https://miamipapanoel.com/reviews

Phone/Text:
305-244-0360

Email:
bookings@miamipapanoel.com

Do not invent reviews, guarantees, exact availability, or nonprofit partnerships. Ask people to request availability.
```

## Weekly Social Posts

```text
Requirements:

- Warm, premium, local, and family-friendly
- Mention Miami-Doral naturally
- Include a soft call to action to request availability

- Do not overuse emojis
```


## Reply To A New Lead

```text

[PASTE CUSTOMER MESSAGE]

Ask for any missing details:
- Date
- Time
- City/address area

Keep it short, friendly, and clear. Include that the visit is confirmed after availability, address, deposit, and final details.
```

## Email Setup Explainer

Use this prompt if the email/DNS setup gets confusing.

```text
Explain these email setup steps to me like I am setting up a small family business website.

Goal:
Make bookings@miamipapanoel.com receive booking form emails.

Current facts:
- Domain: miamipapanoel.com
- DNS provider: Vercel DNS
- There are currently no MX records
- Website is live
- Booking form uses FormSubmit and sends to bookings@miamipapanoel.com

Explain:
1. Why MX records are needed
2. Why FormSubmit activation cannot work until the inbox receives email
3. What records I should copy from Google Workspace, Zoho Mail, or ImprovMX
4. What not to change so the website stays live
5. How to test when it is done
```

## Guardrails

Do not let Gemini:

- Invent fake reviews.
- Claim dates are available before checking.
- Promise nonprofit/free visits without family approval.
- Publish a personal address.
- Share private family information.
- Replace the booking process.
- Change DNS records without checking the provider's exact values.
