# Account Setup Checklist

Create these accounts so the business can receive leads, prove trust, collect payment, and follow up.

## Must-Have Accounts

### Gmail

Suggested email:

- `bookings@miamipapanoel.com` if domain email is available
- `miamipapanoel@gmail.com` if starting free

Use it for all bookings, Vercel, Google Business Profile, Instagram, Facebook, payment apps, and calendar invites.

### Instagram

Suggested handles:

- `@miamipapanoel`
- `@papanolemiami`
- `@papanoel.doral`
- `@santamiamipapanoel`

Profile name:

`Miami Papa Noel | Santa Visits`

### Facebook Page

Page name:

`Miami Papa Noel`

Category:

`Party Entertainment Service`, `Event Planner`, or `Local Service`

### WhatsApp Business

Use WhatsApp Business for quick family and Spanish-speaking client communication.

Set quick replies for:

- Package information
- Availability request
- Deposit request
- Gift instructions
- Review request

### Google Business Profile

Business name:

`Miami Papa Noel`

Category:

`Entertainer`, `Party Entertainment Service`, or closest available category

Service area:

Miami, Doral, Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, Coral Gables, and nearby areas.

### Booking Form

Start with the website `book.html`. Later, connect one of these:

- Google Forms
- Tally
- Typeform
- Formspree
- Jotform

### Calendar

Use Google Calendar named:

`Miami Papa Noel Bookings`

Color code:

- Hold
- Deposit paid
- Confirmed
- Completed

### Payments

Use one or two easy payment methods:

- Zelle
- Cash App
- Venmo
- Square
- Stripe payment link

Require a deposit to hold the date.
