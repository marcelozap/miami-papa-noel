# Summer Client Engine

Goal: create real conversations for Papa Noel during June and July, then turn those conversations into December bookings, partner referrals, and repeat event clients.

The website is proof. The engine is outbound.

## The Summer Offer

Do not pitch "Santa in the summer" as random. Pitch it as one of these clear moments:

1. Camp or daycare Christmas-in-July visit
   - Best for daycares, summer camps, preschools, enrichment programs, and kids gyms.
   - Offer: a short bilingual Santa greeting, photos, and a simple story or activity moment.
   - Best timing: weekday mornings or early afternoons.

2. Christmas-in-July photo pop-up
   - Best for HOAs, apartment communities, restaurants, cafes, boutiques, family photographers, pet groomers, and pet stores.
   - Offer: a 60-90 minute photo-friendly Santa appearance.
   - Best timing: Saturday morning, Sunday afternoon, or weekday resident event.

3. Toy drive or community kickoff
   - Best for nonprofits, churches, clubs, sponsor-supported events, and local businesses that want goodwill.
   - Offer: Santa helps announce the drive, takes photos, greets families, and creates a donation moment.
   - Best timing: late July through early November.

## What To Say In One Sentence

English:

`My dad is Miami Papa Noel, a warm bilingual Santa in Miami-Doral. We are booking Christmas-in-July visits for camps, daycares, pet photos, HOAs, and family businesses, and it can also help reserve December dates early.`

Spanish:

`Mi papa es Miami Papa Noel, un Santa bilingue y calido en Miami-Doral. Estamos reservando visitas de Christmas in July para campamentos, daycares, fotos con mascotas, comunidades y negocios familiares, y tambien ayuda a separar fechas de diciembre temprano.`

## 14-Day Sprint

Run this for 14 days before changing the plan.

Daily minimum:

- Add 10 new prospects to `summer-prospect-starter.csv` or `../lead-tracker.csv`.
- Send 10 first messages.
- Send 5 follow-ups.
- Make 2 calls or WhatsApp messages to the best fits.
- Post 1 story or short reel showing a real Santa moment.

Weekly target:

- 70 prospects added.
- 50 first messages sent.
- 25 follow-ups sent.
- 5 warm conversations.
- 1 paid summer visit, partner pop-up, or December deposit.

## What Counts As A Win

Not every message needs to book immediately. A win is:

- They ask for photos.
- They ask for price.
- They say who handles events.
- They give an email.
- They want December information.
- They agree to a partner call.
- They say "not now" but invite follow-up later.

Log every win with a follow-up date. The follow-up is where most bookings happen.
