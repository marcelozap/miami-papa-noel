What to Prepare

A few building details keep the visit smooth.

Before confirming, share the property name, city, event room, parking, contact person, guest estimate, and whether the visit includes photos, gifts, or a donation table.

  • Choose one staff or board contact for arrival.
  • Confirm parking, gate access, elevator, and loading details.
  • Pick a photo area with enough space for families to wait.
  • Label gifts clearly if Santa will hand them out.
