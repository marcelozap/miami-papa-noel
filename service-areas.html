Parking

Area FAQ

Quick answers before you send the city.

The first request can be simple. Date, city, time, and visit type are enough to start.

Do you visit outside Doral?

Yes. Miami Papa Noel serves Doral, Miami, Hialeah, Kendall, Sweetwater, Miami Lakes, Coral Gables, and nearby communities by request.

Does travel change the price?

Sometimes. Distance, traffic window, visit length, and date can affect the final quote.

Can you do Spanish visits?

Yes. Visits can be in English, Spanish, or a mix of both depending on the family or event.
