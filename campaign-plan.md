# Papa Noel Client Campaign Plan

## Positioning

Papa Noel is a warm, experienced, bilingual Santa for family parties, residential communities, businesses, schools, daycares, nonprofits, toy drives, Christmas novenas, photo sessions, and Christmas Eve gift delivery in Miami-Doral and nearby areas.

The emotional message: he brings calm, joy, patience, and real Christmas magic to children and families.

## Summer Santa Ideas

Summer is the time to book early and create attention before everyone else starts advertising.

- Christmas in July photo pop-up at cafes, bakeries, salons, daycares, and small shops.
- Toy drive kickoff with Santa collecting donations for children.
- Daycare story time: Santa reads a short Christmas story and takes photos.
- Birthday surprise visit: "Santa stopped by early because you are on the nice list."
- Summer family day at apartment complexes and HOAs.
- Pet photos with Santa for groomers, pet shops, and rescue fundraisers.
- Back-to-school kindness visit: Santa reminds kids to be kind, helpful, and brave.
- Sponsor-a-Santa nonprofit package where a business pays for Santa to attend a community event.

## Campaign Calendar

### June

- Build Instagram/Facebook page or refresh existing page.
- Post best 9 photos so the profile looks active.
- Make a short booking form with Google Forms.
- Build lead list of 150 targets.
- Start Christmas in July outreach.

### July

- Run Christmas in July campaign.
- Offer discounted early-bird bookings for December deposits.
- Contact daycares, summer camps, pet groomers, and family restaurants.
- Ask one nonprofit partner for a testimonial or permission to mention collaboration.

### August

- Contact HOAs, apartment complexes, schools, churches, photographers, and party planners.
- Offer "reserve your December date now" messaging.
- Create 2 short videos from photo slideshows.

### September

- Push residential communities and corporate holiday parties.
- Contact event planners and balloon decorators for referral partnerships.
- Post availability calendar updates weekly.

### October

- Increase urgency: weekend dates filling.
- Pitch Christmas Eve gift delivery.
- Ask families to book private visits before December rush.

### November and December

- Post proof from each event, with permission.
- Keep weekday discounts open.
- Ask every client for a review, tagged story, or referral.

## Daily Outreach Routine

- 15 Instagram DMs.
- 5 emails.
- 3 phone calls or WhatsApp messages.
- 1 follow-up to someone from yesterday.
- 1 post or story.
